What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Ownership Mentality

Everyone on our team is empowered to take initiative and ownership of their work. We don't micromanage — we trust our people to lead from their position.

02

Radical Transparency

We communicate directly, share what's working and what isn't, and make decisions together. No politics. No hidden agendas. Just honest dialogue.

03

Excellence Always

We set a high standard in every product we deliver and every role we fill. Average isn't in our vocabulary — and we hire people who feel the same way.

04

Lift As You Climb

Success at Chem USA isn't individual. When one of us wins, all of us win. We actively invest in each other's growth and advancement.

05

Grit & Resilience

This industry is demanding, and so are we. We hire people who are tough, adaptable, and energized by challenges rather than intimidated by them.

06

Community Impact

As a woman-owned enterprise, we're committed to creating opportunities for other women in supply chain, logistics, and industrial sectors.

Our Story

Started With a Vision.
Built With Purpose.

Chem USA was founded in 2014 with a clear mission: provide industrial facilities with superior chemical maintenance products — backed by service that actually shows up.

What started as a bold idea became a full-scale operation, managing manufacturing, supply chain, warehousing, and logistics for three premium product lines reaching all 50 states.

More than a decade later, we're still woman-owned, still growing, and still deeply committed to the belief that the best teams are built on talent, not tradition.
