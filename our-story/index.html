Our Story

From a single idea to a national supply operation — here's how Chem USA came to be, and where we're headed.

How It Began

Built From the Ground Up.

Chem USA was founded in 2014 with a clear mission: provide industrial facilities with superior chemical maintenance products — backed by service that actually shows up.

What started as a bold idea became a full-scale operation, managing manufacturing, supply chain, warehousing, and logistics for three premium product lines reaching all 50 states.

We built this company on the belief that small businesses could compete at the highest level — not by cutting corners, but by outworking, out-serving, and out-caring every competitor in the space.

Our Mission: Deliver Products That Perform. Service That Shows Up.

We believe industrial facilities deserve a supplier they can actually count on — one that picks up the phone, ships on time, and stands behind every product.

Our Journey

A Decade of Growth.

2014

Founded in South Florida

Chem USA launches with a focused mission — bring premium chemical maintenance products to industrial clients who deserve better than what the big distributors were offering.

2016

National Distribution Established

After two years of rapid client growth, Chem USA expands its logistics network to serve clients in all 50 states — building the carrier relationships and fulfillment infrastructure that still power the business today.

2018

Product Line Expansion

Three distinct product lines are solidified — each engineered for the demands of different industrial environments. Quality control and proprietary formulas become a core competitive advantage.

2021

Expanded Operations & Team

Chem USA grows its team and moves into a larger facility to support increasing order volume. The culture of accountability and ownership that defines the company today takes full shape during this period.

Today

Growing Stronger Every Year

More than a decade in, Chem USA continues to grow — expanding our portfolio of companies, deepening client relationships, and building a team of people who care as much about the work as we do.
